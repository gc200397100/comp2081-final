About Us

The Lazy Tulip Cafe is open for breakfast & lunch featuring mindful, healthy food options & organic, fair trade coffee located right downtown Barrie!

Open seven days a week we offer breakfast & lunch focusing on healthy, fresh foods. Everything is made in house including our bread, soups, quiche and all our baked goods offering gluten-free & vegan options on both menus. Coffee is provided by Planet Bean Coffee, freshly roasted in Guelph, Ontario.

Newly expanded we now have more room for larger parties and private events. Stay tuned as the Lazy Tulip continues to evolve into a cafe for the community where you'll find good soulful food, great coffee and a cool vibe!
